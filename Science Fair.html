So, what is Science?

The definition explained

Science, is the study of the world, including us. Everything is connected with it, including history and geography because, science is the fundamental subject. If you don't know science, how will you know the outside world and even your own environment?

Actually, everything is science, even the history of Earth. It's exciting to know about science because it's endless but, it may be quite complicated. Therefore, people usually think science is difficult and impossible because they think that they need special equipment that they don't have. But, it's quite the opposite. You can perform simple experiments and learn more about science, then. So, what are the benefits? First, they'll know all about their environment. Next, they'll know the advantages and disadvantages of it. Finally, it can even help your career, such as a scientist, an engineer or a weather forecaster.

Now, you know what science is. So, you should no longer fell uneasy about it. If you learn science, you can learn everything such as history, geography or everything else.
